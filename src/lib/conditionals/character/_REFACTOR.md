## Gotchas
Some characters Eidolon 3 & 5 have differing +2 levels for their respective Skills.

**The "standard" calcs (Jingliu, Dr. Ratio):**
*Eidolon 3:*
- Ult +2
- Basic +2

*Eidolon 5:*
- Talent +2
- Skill +2

**The Reversed calcs (Topaz, etc.):**
*Eidolon 3:*
- Talent +2
- Skill +2

*Eidolon 5:*
- Ult +2
- Basic +2
